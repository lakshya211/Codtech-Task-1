Shopping Trends Dataset
Dataset Description
The shopping_trends.csv dataset contains detailing customer shopping behavior, transaction details, and demographic information. This dataset can be used to analyze patterns and trends in consumer purchases.

Key Columns:
Customer ID: A unique identifier for each customer.
Age: Age of the customer in years.
Gender: Gender of the customer.
Item Purchased: Specific item bought by the customer.
Category: The category of the purchased item (e.g., Clothing, Footwear).
Purchase Amount (USD): The monetary value of the purchase in USD.
Location: Geographical location of the customer.
Size: Size of the purchased item (e.g., Small, Medium, Large).
Color: Color of the purchased item.
Season: Season in which the item was bought.
Review Rating: Customer's rating for the product (out of 5).
Subscription Status: Whether the customer has a subscription.
Payment Method: Method used for the transaction (e.g., Credit Card, PayPal).
Shipping Type: Mode of shipping selected for the purchase.
Discount Applied: Whether a discount was used for the purchase.
Promo Code Used: If a promotional code was applied.
Previous Purchases: Total number of past purchases by the customer.
Preferred Payment Method: Payment method frequently used by the customer.
Frequency of Purchases: How often the customer shops (e.g., Weekly, Annually).
Possible Uses
Consumer Behavior Analysis: Understanding purchasing habits based on age, gender, and location.
Sales Trends: Identifying popular categories and products.
Marketing Insights: Exploring the effectiveness of discounts and promo codes.
Operational Insights: Analyzing payment and shipping preferences
